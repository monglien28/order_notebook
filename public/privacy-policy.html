Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Notebook For Mee, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and website to order your customized notebooks.

Information We Collect

To provide you with the best service, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Details: Name, mobile number, email address, and delivery location.
  • Order Information: Details regarding your notebook customizations, files uploaded, and purchase history.
  • Security Data: OTPs and authentication tokens to ensure secure login.
  • Device Data: Information about your device to improve app performance and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Processing and delivering your customized orders efficiently.
  • Verifying your identity through OTP-based authentication.
  • Providing customer support and resolving issues.
  • Improving our app, website, and overall service quality.
  • Sending important updates regarding your orders.

Data Sharing

We respect your privacy. We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information is shared only with trusted third-party services necessary for operations:

  • Logistics Partners: To ensure your order reaches your doorstep.
  • Technical Infrastructure: Cloud and analytics services (e.g., Firebase) to run the app.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law enforcement or legal authorities.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored securely.

User Rights

You have full control over your data. You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

Children’s Privacy

While our services may be used by children (e.g., for school notebooks), we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this page occasionally.
